    Project Proposal

    We want to create an application that lets users analyze Olin courses based on registration data. The goal is to collect said (anonymous) data from the registrar’s office and compile it into a usable format. Then, several course metrics like relative difficulty, importance to graduation, and popularity with students will be created. After analyzing the resulting data set and producing a report, we’d make the data/metrics exportable in a useful format so that future researchers can carry on our work.
